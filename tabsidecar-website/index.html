Session Recovery

Windows that survive a restart.

If Chrome closes for any reason, a crash, a force-quit, or a normal restart, your windows reopen the way you left them.

  • Restarts are detected automatically
  • Each window is matched to its saved state
  • Works even when Chrome's own restore doesn't
Running

Chrome quits, then every window is restored

Auto-Park

Close the clutter. Keep every tab.

When a tab sits untouched past a timer you set, TabSidecar closes it and adds it to a list you can search and reopen.

  • You choose the timer, from an hour to a few weeks
  • Parked tabs are searchable and reopen in one click
  • Pinned tabs are left alone

Window Management

Take control of every window.

  • Save a window and reopen the whole set later

Questions

Common questions.

Is it really free?
Yes. It's free while in beta. We're working on a paid Pro plan with AI features, but everything available now will stay free.
Do parked tabs get deleted?
No. Parking a tab closes it but saves it to a searchable list. One click brings it back, and pinned tabs are never parked.
Do I need an account?
No. There's no sign-up. TabSidecar runs locally in your browser.
Will it close tabs I'm actively using?
Only tabs you haven't touched since the timer you set. Pinned tabs are never closed.
Does it survive a Chrome crash?
Yes. That's Session Recovery: window layouts are saved in the background and restored when Chrome reopens.
Can I back up my data?
Yes. Export your tabs, labels, filters, and settings to JSON or CSV, and import them back later.
